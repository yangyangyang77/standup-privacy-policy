Privacy Policy for StandUp

We believe in complete transparency and respect for your privacy. Here's everything you need to know about how StandUp handles data:

What We Store

  • Only your break count is stored
  • It's saved locally on your device
  • You can see this number in the extension popup
  • This data is cleared when you uninstall the extension

What We Don't Do

  • We don't collect any personal information
  • We don't track your browsing
  • We don't use cookies
  • We don't send any data anywhere
  • We don't use any third-party services

How the Extension Works

  • Works completely offline
  • Uses Chrome's built-in storage for your break count
  • Shows reminders using popup windows
  • That's it!
